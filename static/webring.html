Webring

To add your name to the webring, create a pull request to the /static/webring.js file in this repository. There's a list of members there, and you should include your username (typically your name, but it can be anything, just unique) and url.

Adding your name to the webring implies that you will also add the webring links to your site.

To make sure your username is unique, here's a small widget that should help out! It tells you if your username is already taken or not. In addition, there's also a generator for the webring links that you can add :P
